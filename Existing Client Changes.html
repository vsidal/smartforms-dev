What happens next?

You will need to provide documentation to verify the identity of the new legal entity as part of our KYC process. This process is important because it helps us verify the identity of your business and its owners, ensuring compliance with the Money Laundering Regulations. You will receive a link to an online form to provide your details.

Depending on your company's legal form, we typically request information such as the company name and registration number, business activity description, details of the directors and owners (names, address, dates of birth, contact details). You will also be asked to complete a new Direct Debit Mandate.

You can save your progress at any time by creating an account, allowing you to complete the form at your convenience.

Once you've submitted the form, one or more key individuals of the company will receive an email or SMS with a secure link to complete the identity verification process. Follow the instructions provided in the link, which will include:

  • Uploading a photo of a government-issued ID (such as a passport or driver's license);
  • Taking a selfie;
  • Providing us with financial information on your business through online banking.
