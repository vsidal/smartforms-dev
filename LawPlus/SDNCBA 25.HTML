Scope of work included in this Agreement

All the work we do on your claim for compensation

Scope of work excluded from this Agreement

The issue of court proceedings and any work consequent to the issue of court proceedings.

Paying us if you Win

If we win your claim, you will pay us a fee based on the compensation that we recover on your behalf, as outlined in the fee caps under the SRA Claims Management Fees Rules 2024, plus applicable VAT at the standard rate (currently 20% of our charges). The fees are calculated as follows:

  • For redress awarded between £1 - £1,499: 30% or £420, whichever is lower
  • For redress awarded between £1,500 - £9,999: 28% or £2,500, whichever is lower
  • For redress awarded between £10,000 - £24,999: 25% or £5,000, whichever is lower
  • For redress awarded between £25,000 - £49,999: 20% or £7,500, whichever is lower
  • For redress awarded £50,000 or above: 15% or £10,000, whichever is lower
  1. if you lose we will not earn anything
  2. your case is complex and involves specialised knowledge on our part
  3. the time we spend on your business
  4. the volume of documents
  5. the amount of money involved
  6. the importance of this matter to you
  7. what we consider to be a fair and reasonable charge for the work we will do on your case
Paying us if you Lose

If you lose your claim, you do not pay us anything

Paying us if you end the Agreement

You can end this Agreement at any time. If you end this Agreement before the claim is won or lost you then are liable to pay us for the work we have done on your claim at an hourly rate of £300 per hour for each hour of work we have done. You agree to pay us for routine letters or telephone calls lasting less than 6 minutes at 1/10th of the hourly rate. If they take longer they will be charged at the appropriate proportion of the hourly rate. VAT will be added to this sum.

Paying us if we end the Agreement

We can end this Agreement if you do not keep to your responsibilities as set out in this Agreement. You then are liable to pay us for the work we have done on your claim at an hourly rate of £300 per hour for each hour of work we have done. You agree to pay us for routine letters or telephone calls lasting less than 6 minutes at 1/10th of the hourly rate. If they take longer they will be charged at the appropriate proportion of the hourly rate. VAT will be added to this sum.

We can end this Agreement if you reject our advice about accepting a settlement. You then are liable to pay us for the work we have done on your claim at an hourly rate of £300 per hour for each hour of work we have done. You agree to pay us for routine letters or telephone calls lasting less than 6 minutes at 1/10th of the hourly rate. If they take longer they will be charged at the appropriate proportion of the hourly rate. VAT will be added to this sum.

We can end this Agreement if we believe that you are unlikely to win. You do not then have to pay us anything.

Your responsibilities

Your responsibilities are to

  1. Give us clear instructions promptly which allow us to work on your claim properly.
  2. Not to ask us to work in an improper or unreasonable way.
  3. Not to deliberately mislead us or lie or exaggerate your claim.
  4. Co-operate with us in pursuing your claim.
Our responsibilities

Our responsibilities are to

  1. Always act in your best interests, subject to our professional obligations.
  2. Explain to you the risks and benefits of pursuing your claim.
  3. Give you our best advice about whether to accept any offer of settlement.
  4. Give you the best information possible about the likely costs of your claim.
Paying us from your compensation

If you win you pay us the agreed percentage of the compensation that we recover on your behalf and any VAT. You agree that we may receive the money you recover. If the money is sent to you, you agree to pay into a designated account any cheque received by your or by us and made payable to you. Out of the money you agree to let us take first our share of the money. You take the rest.

Billing you

We will send you requests for payment on account of disbursements as the claim progresses. We will send you a final statute bill of our fees at the conclusion of your claim.

Rights to assessment under section 70 of the Solicitors Act 1974

You have a right to have our final statute bill assessed by the Court under section 70 of the Solicitors Act 1974. Strict time limits apply for you to make any application for such an assessment. Those time limits are specified in section 70 of the Solicitors Act 1974 and if in doubt you should seek immediate legal advice.

Death

This Agreement automatically ends if you die before your claim is concluded. We will be entitled to recover our fees from your estate. Your estate is then liable to pay us for the work we have done on your claim at an hourly rate of £300 per hour for each hour of work we have done. You agree to pay us for routine letters or telephone calls lasting less than 6 minutes at 1/10th of the hourly rate. If they take longer they will be charged at the appropriate proportion of the hourly rate. VAT will be added to this sum. If your personal representatives wish to continue your claim we may offer them a new non-contentious business agreement.

What happens after this Agreement ends

After this Agreement ends, if any money is owing to us from you, we have the right to preserve our lien and may refuse to release documents to you or any new solicitor until that money has been paid.

Explanation of words used

(a) Our charges

The percentage share of compensation we recover on your behalf that you agree to pay us with VAT; or alternatively a sum calculated at an hourly rate that you become liable to pay us under this Agreement with VAT.

(b) Claim

Your demand for compensation

(c) Compensation

Money that we recover on your behalf

(d) Lien

Our right to keep all papers, documents, money, or other property held on your behalf until all money due to us is paid. A lien may be applied after this Agreement ends.

(e) Lose

Your claim for compensation has failed or you have stopped it on our advice.

(f) Win

Your claim for compensation is decided in your favour by an award or settlement of money.
